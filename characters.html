Intrepid Pro is the name the team gives themselves when they formally commit to stopping Ella's father. Five boys, one additional girl. The "Pro" signals intent, they consider themselves professional operatives. They like the sound of it. So does the reader.

Ratio: Ella + Ari vs. Lincoln, Joop, Hemi, Jamaal, Mitch, 2 girls : 5 boys. Ella's outsider dynamic is built in from the start.

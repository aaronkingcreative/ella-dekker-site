Phase Three · The Belt · Books VIII–X
Book VIII Ella Dekker and the Belt Pirates Working Title
Ceres Asteroid Belt
Ceres Station. Space pirates with their own code. Bas Jan trying to merge two dangerous worlds into one. Ella caught between forces she doesn't yet fully understand.
Ella is caught between two dangerous worlds.
Book IX Ella Dekker and the Ceres Blackout Working Title
Ceres Interior Ceres Exterior
A power outage. Sabotage. iPro deploys drones like a financial investment — a battle fought in code and credit, not fists. Fighting without fighting.

Book X Ella Dekker and the Final Orbit Working Title
Ceres Open Belt Space
The merger is complete. Bas Jan is removed. The victory Ella worked toward across ten books arrives — and immediately creates something worse. Season One ends.
Ella loses her target. Gains a bigger one.

Season One is a complete story. Ella arrives. Ella grows. Ella wins — and loses. The solar system expands book by book, from Earth orbit to Saturn's rings to the Asteroid Belt. Every destination is earned. Season Two is already waiting on the other side of the belt.

Earth Intrepid Arrival
She left Earth to be with her father on the Intrepid. What she found was more dangerous, and more exciting than anything she planned for.
Ella finds a world and loses her plan.

Intrepid Near-Earth Orbit
When Professor Agasa's work comes under threat from a rival, Ella and the team take to near-Earth orbit to outmaneuver him.
Ella saves Professor Agasa. Accidentally arms her enemy.
Book III Ella Dekker and the Lunar Rescue Working Title
Intrepid Moon
A school field trip to the lunar base goes nothing like the itinerary promised. Ella and Professor Agasa come to the rescue.
Ella's first real excursion away from the Intrepid.
Phase Two · Into the Deep · Books IV–V
Book IV Ella Dekker and the Starliner Academy Working Title
Starliner Transit Deep Space
A months-long voyage to Saturn aboard a deep-space star liner starts threatening the lives of everyone onboard when the ship's robotic systems start failing mid-transit. Ella and the team have no choice but to help them fix it. A strange language is discovered by the team that ultimately leads to a repeating signal carrying a message no one can decipher: a Ghost Frequency.
Everyone is further from home than they have ever been. Something is broadcasting in the dark.
Book V Ella Dekker and the Ghost Frequency Working Title
Saturn Orbital
They've arrived at Saturn but the strange signal from the starliner remains a mystery. Cracking the Ghost Frequency means working with someone unexpected: a scientist living at the edge of the ringed world.
The search that started on Earth ends at Saturn. Some discoveries only lead to different problems.
Phase Three · The Belt · Books VI–VII
Book VI Ella Dekker and the Belt Pirates Working Title
Ceres Asteroid Belt
The Ghost Frequency pointed here: Ceres Station deep in the Asteroid Belt. Two dangerous factions are circling each other, when the station's power goes out. Now trapped, some long-overdue conversations finally happen in the dark.
Ella came to stop a collision. She ends up in the middle of one.
Book VII Ella Dekker and the Agasa Protocol Working Title
Ceres Open Belt Space
Two factions want Professor Agasa's drone technology and they're willing to fight a war in the belt to get it. Agasa's answer is the Protocol. The Solar System Authority gains a powerful ally in Agasa.
Ella didn't come to space to change the solar system. She's about to.

Season One is a complete story. Ella arrives. Ella grows. Ella wins. But the solar system is never quite the same. Earth orbit to Saturn's rings to the Asteroid Belt. Every destination earned. Season Two is waiting on the other side of the consequences and benefits of the Agasa Protocol.

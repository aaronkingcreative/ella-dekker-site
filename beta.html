What Beta Reading Involves
01
Read the Current Draft
You'll receive the current working draft — not the complete book, but the segment ready for reader feedback at that stage. Format of your choice. What you receive will be clearly described when the invitation goes out.
02
Share Your Reactions
A short questionnaire after reading covers what worked, what confused you, what you loved, and what you'd skip. No formal review required. Honest reactions are all that matters.
03
Get Early Access
Beta readers who complete the process will receive updates as the book progresses toward publication. We'll do our best to show appreciation — the specifics depend on how the publication path unfolds.
